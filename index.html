How it works

Three components, one pipeline

Each component runs sequentially — profile in, prioritised lead out.

Component 01
Lead scorer
Random Forest + Gradient Boosting ensemble. 12 LinkedIn signals → Hot / Warm / Cold label with 0–100 priority score and confidence %.
scikit-learn
Component 02
Reply predictor
TF-IDF + GBM regression. Scores your message quality, predicts reply probability %, and gives specific tips to improve it before sending.
NLP + GBM
Component 03
Personalizer
Reads bio keywords, title, and industry to craft a specific opening hook. Detects signals like "scaling", "outbound", "B2B" and adapts the message tone.
NLP engine
